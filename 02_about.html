About ZeroWaste

Empowering communities through food donations and reducing waste for a better tomorrow.

Our Mission

ZeroWaste is dedicated to connecting donors and recipients, ensuring that no food goes to waste. We aim to make food accessible to those in need, whether they are individuals, shelters, or NGOs.

Our Vision

We envision a world where food waste is minimized, and every individual or organization in need can easily access food through a seamless, transparent platform.

How It Works

  1. Donors list food they want to donate using a simple form.
  2. Recipients (NGOs, shelters, individuals) can search for available food near them and request what they need.
  3. Donors and recipients coordinate pickup or delivery of the donated food.
  4. Everyone can track donations and requests through our platform in real-time.
